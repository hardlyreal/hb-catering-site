Menu

Meats (Select 2)

All of our poutry and meats are smoked utilizing a combination of hard woods and non-seasoned cherry wood.

BBQ Pork Loin

Description

Boneless Pork Loin that is marinated in a variety of seasonings for a 24 hour period. It is served thinly, hand sliced and covered in our BBQ sauce.

Smoked Bone-In Chicken Thighs

Description

Fresh chicken thighs that are soaked in a mixture of seasonings, garlic, and hot peppers. We can serve the final product either with our BBQ sauce or smoked with seasonings.

Angus Top Round Beef

Description

Trimmed and marinated for 24 hours and then slowly smoked to ensure a tender final product.

Smoked Boneless Turkey Breast

Description

One of our all time best sellers features boneless turkey breasts marinated in a mixture of olive oil and seasonings 24 hours prior to smoking. The result is a tender and delicious product that has made it one of our most popular selections.

French Cut Lamb Ribs

Description

Also known locally as Lamb Loli-Pops, these are a delicious addition to any menu. We lightly sear the ribs over a hard wood fire and then smoke them to complete the process. The result is a tender and addictive item that will have your guests coming back for more.
